This mod makes it possible to have a super smash in Terraria. No matter enemies and bosses, all of them can be used to fight.
None of the items in mod can be obtained, so it is recommended to play with Hero's Mod or Cheat Sheet Mod.
Do not support multiplayer mode.
Have fun! :)
